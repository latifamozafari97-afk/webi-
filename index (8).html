Our Story

Local Bistro was born from a simple belief: the best meals start with the best ingredients, sourced from the farms and waters closest to our kitchen door.

Every season, our chef reimagines the menu to showcase what's fresh, partnering with local growers and artisans to bring honest, soulful food to your table.

Whether it's a weeknight dinner, a celebration, or a quiet glass of wine at the bar — we're glad you're here.

— Chef Margaux & the LB team
